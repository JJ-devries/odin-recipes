Green Curry

Description

The single most requested recipe is finally here!! We love the color, the spiciness, and how fragrant the creamy sauce is. Along with Pad Thai, Chicken Satay and Thai Red Curry, green curry is the gold standard by which we rate Thai restaurants. It’s the curry we sweat and swear our way through, guzzling down water and wiping our brows when we confidently tell the waiter “give it to us the way it should be – extra spicy!!” Luckily, when we make it at home, we can dial back the spiciness substantially. Whether made with curry paste in a jar OR homemade green curry paste!

Ingredients

  • Coconut milk
  • Chicken or vegetable broth
  • Chicken
  • Asian (Japanese) Eggplant
  • Snow peas
  • Sugar
  • Fish sauce
  • Kaffir Lime leaves
  • Thai Basil
  • Lime juice

Steps

  1. fry off curry paste
  2. add broth
  3. add Coconut milk and Kaffir lime
  4. add chicken
  5. cook for 10 min
  6. add eggplant and snow peas
  7. add thai basil and lime juice
  8. serve with rice
